  Key Concepts: Parallel Lines and Transversals
Overview

Key Definitions
What is a transversal?
Transversal A transversal is a Line that intersects two or more coplanar lines at different points. When a transversal crosses two lines, it creates 8 angles. The other options — 'A line parallel to two other lines', 'A line segment with two endpoints', and 'A ray that bisects an angle' — are related concepts but do not correctly answer this specific question.
