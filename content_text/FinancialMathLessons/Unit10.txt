--- Lesson 10.1: Review of Key Formulas (Summary) ---

  Key Concepts: Review of Key Formulas
Key Financial Math FormulasTime Value of Money  • FV = PV × (1 + r)^n  • PV = FV / (1 + r)^n  • Rule of 72: Years to double ≈ 72 / rateBusiness Math  • Break-even (units) = Fixed Costs / (Price − Variable Cost)  • Profit Margin = Net Profit / Revenue × 100  • ROI = (Gain − Cost) / Cost × 100Investment Math  • NPV = −Investment + Σ(CF_t / (1+r)^t)  • Sharpe Ratio = (Return − Risk-free) / Std Dev  • WACC = (% Debt × Cost of Debt × (1−Tax)) + (% Equity × Cost of Equity)

  Key Concepts: Capstone: Personal Financial Plan
Capstone: Build Your Personal Financial PlanPlan Components  • 1. Net Worth Statement: Assets − Liabilities = Net Worth.  • 2.
