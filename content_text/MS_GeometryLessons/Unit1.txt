Distance Formula The distance between two points (x₁, y₁) and (x₂, y₂) in the coordinate Plane is: d = √[(x₂ − x₁)² + (y₂ − y₁)²] This formula comes from the Pythagorean Theorem. The midpoint of a segment with endpoints (x₁,y₁) and (x₂,y₂) is M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2). The midpoint divides the segment into two equal parts.
