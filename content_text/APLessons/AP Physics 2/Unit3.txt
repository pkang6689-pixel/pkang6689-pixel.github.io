     A: A region of space around a magnet or moving charge where magnetic forces are exerted

     A: A wave that consists of oscillating electric and magnetic fields perpendicular to each other and to the direction of propagation

  6. Q: Define: Capacitor
     A: A device that stores electrical energy by accumulating electric charge on two conducting plates

  7. Q: Define: Resistance
     A: The opposition to the flow of electric current through a material, measured in ohms

     A: The phenomenon where two or more waves overlap and combine to create a resultant wave
