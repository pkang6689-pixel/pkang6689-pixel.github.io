--- Lesson 10.2: Operations with Radicals (Summary) ---

  Key Concepts: Operations with Radicals
Add/Subtract radicals: only combine like radicals (same radicand). Example: 3тИЪ5 + 2тИЪ5 = 5тИЪ5.

Multiply radicals: тИЪa ┬╖ тИЪb = тИЪ(ab). Example: тИЪ3 ┬╖ тИЪ12 = тИЪ36 = 6.

--- Lesson 10.3: Rational Exponents (Summary) ---

  Key Concepts: Rational Exponents
A rational exponent a^(m/n) = тБ┐тИЪ(aс╡Р) = (тБ┐тИЪa)с╡Р.

--- Lesson 10.4: Solving Radical Equations (Summary) ---

  Key Concepts: Solving Radical Equations
To solve a radical equation, isolate the radical, then raise both sides to the appropriate power.

For square roots: square both sides. For cube roots: cube both sides.

Always check for extraneous solutions тАФ squaring can introduce false solutions.

Example: тИЪ(x + 3) = 5 тЖТ x + 3 = 25 тЖТ x = 22. Check: тИЪ25 = 5 тЬУ
