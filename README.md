# ArisEdu

## 🎓 STEM Education Platform — For Students, By Students

[arisedu.com](https://arisedu.com)

ArisEdu is a comprehensive STEM education platform covering **32 courses** across Middle School, High School, and AP levels. Featuring interactive lessons with summaries, flashcards, quizzes, practice problems, 25 arcade games, teacher analytics, multi-language support, and Firebase-backed progress tracking.

### ✨ Key Features

- **Personalized Dashboard** — course progress, recent activity, welcome section
- **Interactive Lessons** — summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and practice per lesson
- **25 Arcade Games** — 2048, Tetris, Pacman, Snake, Platformer, Craft 2D, and more
- **Teacher Analytics** — Chart.js-powered performance dashboards
- **Community Forums** — post and comment on discussions
- **Multi-Language Support** — English, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi (72 translation files)
- **Theme Options** — Light, Dark, and Aurora modes
- **AI Assistant** — built-in AI helper
- **Global Search** — search across all courses and content
- **Firebase Auth & Firestore** — email/password login, cloud-synced progress
- **Middle School Adaptations** — easier quizzes (5 questions, 3 attempts), curated flashcards, MS-appropriate video selection, difficulty-tuned practice games

### 🚀 Getting Started

1. Visit [arisedu.com](https://arisedu.com)
2. Create an account or log in
3. Select your level (Middle School, High School, or AP)
4. Choose a subject and navigate through units and lessons
5. Complete quizzes, practice problems, and flashcards
6. Track your progress on the dashboard
7. Earn arcade tokens and play games in the arcade
