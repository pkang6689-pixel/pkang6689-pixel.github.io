Lesson 7.7: Systems with Quadratics

Key Concepts: Systems with Quadratics

A system with a linear and quadratic equation can have 0, 1, or 2 solutions.

Solve by substitution: set the linear equation equal to the quadratic and solve the resulting equation.

Graphically: solutions are the intersection points of the line and parabola.

A system of two quadratics can also have 0, 1, or 2 solutions.
