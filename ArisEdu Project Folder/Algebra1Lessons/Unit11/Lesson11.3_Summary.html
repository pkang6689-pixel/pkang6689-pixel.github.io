Lesson 11.3: Introduction to Series

Key Concepts: Introduction to Series

A series is the sum of the terms of a sequence.

Arithmetic series sum: S(n) = n/2 · (a(1) + a(n)) or S(n) = n/2 · (2a(1) + (n−1)d).

Geometric series sum (finite): S(n) = a(1)(1 − rⁿ)/(1 − r), where r ≠ 1.

Sigma notation (Σ) provides a compact way to write a series: Σ from i=1 to n of a(i).
