Lesson 5.7: Transformations of Exponential Functions

Key Concepts: Transformations of Exponential Functions

y = a · b^(x−h) + k: h shifts horizontally, k shifts vertically, a stretches/reflects.

The horizontal asymptote shifts from y = 0 to y = k.

Reflection: −a reflects over the x-axis; b^(−x) reflects over the y-axis.

Transformations of exponential functions follow the same rules as other function transformations.
