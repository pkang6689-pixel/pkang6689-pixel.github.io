Lesson 8.3: Dividing Polynomials

Key Concepts: Dividing Polynomials

Long division of polynomials follows the same process as long division of numbers.

Steps: (1) Divide the leading terms. (2) Multiply and subtract. (3) Bring down the next term. (4) Repeat.

Synthetic division is a shortcut when dividing by (x − c). Use only the coefficients.

The Remainder Theorem: when P(x) is divided by (x − c), the remainder equals P(c).
