Lesson 12.2: Independent & Dependent Events

Key Concepts: Independent & Dependent Events

Events are independent if the outcome of one does NOT affect the other. P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B).

Events are dependent if the outcome of one affects the other. P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A).

Drawing with replacement → independent. Drawing without replacement → dependent.

P(B|A) is the probability of B given that A has already occurred.
