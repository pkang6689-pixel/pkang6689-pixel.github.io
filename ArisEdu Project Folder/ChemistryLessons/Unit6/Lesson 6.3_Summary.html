Lesson 6.3: Redox Reactions Summary

Key Concepts: Redox Reactions

Core Definitions

  • Oxidation: Loss of electrons (or gain of oxygen); oxidation number increases.
  • Reduction: Gain of electrons (or loss of oxygen); oxidation number decreases.
  • Redox Reaction: A reaction where oxidation and reduction occur simultaneously.
  • Oxidizing Agent: The substance that is reduced (gains electrons); causes oxidation in another substance.
  • Reducing Agent: The substance that is oxidized (loses electrons); causes reduction in another substance.
  • OIL RIG: Memory aid: Oxidation Is Loss, Reduction Is Gain (of electrons).
