Lesson 1.7 Heterogeneous And Homogeneous Mixtures Summary

Key Concepts: Heterogeneous & Homogeneous Mixtures

Core Definitions

  • a homogeneous mixture: A mixture that is uniform throughout
  • a heterogeneous mixture: A mixture that is not uniform throughout
  • Is salt water homogeneous or heterogeneous: Homogeneous
  • Is a salad homogeneous or heterogeneous: Heterogeneous
  • another name for a homogeneous mixture: Solution
  • Is trail mix homogeneous or heterogeneous: Heterogeneous
  • Is air a homogeneous or heterogeneous mixture: Homogeneous
  • Is orange juice with pulp homogeneous or heterogeneous: Heterogeneous
  • Is brass (alloy) homogeneous or heterogeneous: Homogeneous
  • Can heterogeneous mixtures be separated by filtration: Yes, often locally separated
