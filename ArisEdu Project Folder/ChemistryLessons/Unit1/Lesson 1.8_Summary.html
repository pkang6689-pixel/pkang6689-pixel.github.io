Lesson 1.8 Endothermic And Exothermic Reactions Summary

Key Concepts: Endothermic & Exothermic Reactions

Core Definitions

  • an exothermic reaction: A reaction that releases heat/energy to the surroundings
  • an endothermic reaction: A reaction that absorbs heat/energy from the surroundings
  • Does the temperature increase or decrease in an exothermic reaction: Increase
  • Does the temperature increase or decrease in an endothermic reaction: Decrease
  • Is melting ice endothermic or exothermic: Endothermic
  • Is freezing water endothermic or exothermic: Exothermic
  • Is burning a candle endothermic or exothermic: Exothermic
  • Is photosynthesis endothermic or exothermic: Endothermic
  • Is cellular respiration endothermic or exothermic: Exothermic
  • Is cooking an egg endothermic or exothermic: Endothermic
