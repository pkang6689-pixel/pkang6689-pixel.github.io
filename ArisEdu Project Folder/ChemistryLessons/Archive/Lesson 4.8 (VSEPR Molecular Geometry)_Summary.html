Lesson 5.8: VSEPR Theory Molecular Geometry Summary

Key Concepts: ⭐ VSEPR Molecule Shapes

Core Definitions

  • What does VSEPR stand for: Valence Shell Electron Pair Repulsion
  • Shape of CH4 (4 bonds, 0 lone pairs): Tetrahedral
  • Shape of H2O (2 bonds, 2 lone pairs): Bent
  • Shape of CO2 (2 double bonds): Linear
  • Shape of NH3 (3 bonds, 1 lone pair): Trigonal Pyramidal
