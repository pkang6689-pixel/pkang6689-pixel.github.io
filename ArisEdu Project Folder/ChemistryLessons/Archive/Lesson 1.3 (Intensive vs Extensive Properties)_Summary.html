Lesson 1.3 Intensive And Extensive Properties Summary

Key Concepts: Intensive & Extensive Properties

Core Definitions

  • an intensive property: A property that does NOT depend on the amount of matter
  • an extensive property: A property that DOES depend on the amount of matter
  • Is density intensive or extensive: Intensive
  • Is mass intensive or extensive: Extensive
  • Is boiling point intensive or extensive: Intensive
  • Is volume intensive or extensive: Extensive
  • Is color intensive or extensive: Intensive
  • Is solubility intensive or extensive: Intensive
  • Is length intensive or extensive: Extensive
  • Does cutting a wire change its conductivity: No, conductivity is intensive
