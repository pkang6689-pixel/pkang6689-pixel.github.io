Lesson 8.1: Kinetic Molecular Theory Summary

Key Concepts: Kinetic Molecular Theory

Core Definitions

  • Assumption 1 of KMT: Gas particles are small, hard spheres with insignificant volume
  • Assumption 2 of KMT: Motion is rapid, constant, and random
  • Assumption 3 of KMT: Collisions are perfectly elastic
  • What causes gas pressure: Collisions of particles with container walls
