Lesson 3.8: Nuclear Chemistry Stability Radioactivity Summary

Key Concepts: Radioactivity & Stability

Core Definitions

  • alpha decay: Emission of a Helium nucleus (2 protons, 2 neutrons)
  • beta decay: Emission of an electron
  • gamma radiation: High energy electromagnetic radiation
  • What stops alpha particles: Paper or skin
  • What stops gamma rays: Thick lead or concrete
