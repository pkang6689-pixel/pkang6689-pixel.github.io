Lesson 4.6: Periodic Trends Atomic Radius Summary

Key Concepts: Periodic Trends

Core Definitions

  • Trend for atomic radius across a period: Decreases (left to right)
  • Trend for atomic radius down a group: Increases (top to bottom)
  • Trend for electronegativity across a period: Increases
  • Most electronegative element: Fluorine
  • Trend for ionization energy down a group: Decreases
