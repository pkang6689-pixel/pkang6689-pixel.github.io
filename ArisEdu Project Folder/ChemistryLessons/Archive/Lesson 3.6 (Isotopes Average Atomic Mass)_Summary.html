Lesson 3.4: Isotopes Average Atomic Mass Summary

Key Concepts: Isotopes & Radioisotopes

Core Definitions

  • What are isotopes: Atoms of same element with different numbers of neutrons
  • Do isotopes have same atomic number: Yes
  • Do isotopes have same mass number: No
  • Carbon-12 and Carbon-14 differ in number of...: Neutrons
  • a half-life: Time for half of a radioactive sample to decay
