Lesson 5B.1: Covalent Bonds

Key Concepts: Covalent Bonds

Core Definitions

  • Covalent bonds involve _______ of electrons.: Sharing

  • Are covalent compounds usually electrolytes: No
  • Do covalent compounds have high or low melting points: Low (usually)
