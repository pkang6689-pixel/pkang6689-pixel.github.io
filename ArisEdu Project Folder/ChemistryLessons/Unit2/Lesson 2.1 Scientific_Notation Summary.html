Lesson 2.1: Scientific Notation Summary

Key Concepts: Scientific Notation

Core Definitions

  • Convert 0.005 to scientific notation.: 5 x 10^-3
  • Convert 45000 to scientific notation.: 4.5 x 10^4
  • What does a negative exponent indicate: A number between 0 and 1
  • What does a positive exponent indicate: A number greater than 10
  • Convert 2.3 x 10^2 to standard form.: 230
  • Convert 8.9 x 10^-1 to standard form.: 0.89
  • Standard form for 1.0 x 10^0: 1
