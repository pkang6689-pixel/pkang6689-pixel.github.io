Lesson 1.1: Review of Algebra (factoring, radicals, rational expressions) - Summary

Key Concepts: Review of Algebra (factoring, radicals, rational expressions)

Overview

This lesson covers the fundamental measures of center used to analyze and summarize data sets. You'll learn how to calculate and apply the mean, median, and mode, and understand when each is most appropriate.

Key Definitions

What is 'factoring' in algebra?

Factoring is the process of rewriting an expression as a product of its factors, essentially the reverse of expanding or distributing.

What is the 'greatest common factor' (GCF) of a polynomial?

The GCF is the largest factor (including coefficients and variables) that divides into every term of the polynomial without a remainder.

What is a 'rational expression'?

A rational expression is a ratio (fraction) of two polynomials, such as (x² + 1)/(x − 3).

What is the 'least common denominator' (LCD) when adding rational expressions?

The LCD is the least common multiple of all denominators, providing the simplest shared denominator for combining fractions.

What is a 'perfect square trinomial'?

A perfect square trinomial has the form a² + 2ab + b² = (a + b)² or a² − 2ab + b² = (a − b)².

An engineer needs to simplify the expression (x² − 16)/(x + 4) to calculate a load factor. What is the simplified result?

Factor the numerator as a difference of squares: (x + 4)(x − 4)/(x + 4) = x − 4, for x ≠ −4.

A physics student must simplify √72 meters when reporting a measurement. What is the simplified form?

√72 = √(36 · 2) = √36 · √2 = 6√2 meters.

A square courtyard has an area of (x² − 10x + 25) square meters. What is the length of one side?

x² − 10x + 25 is a perfect square trinomial: (x − 5)². The side length is (x − 5) meters.

A scientist calculates a ratio of concentrations as 5/(3 + √2) mg/L. After rationalizing the denominator, what is the expression?

Multiply by the conjugate: 5(3 − √2)/[(3 + √2)(3 − √2)] = 5(3 − √2)/(9 − 2) = 5(3 − √2)/7.

Two pipes fill a tank. Pipe A fills 1/(x + 2) of the tank per hour and Pipe B fills 1/(x − 1) per hour. What is their combined rate using the LCD?

LCD = (x + 2)(x − 1). Rewrite: (x − 1)/((x + 2)(x − 1)) + (x + 2)/((x + 2)(x − 1)) = (x − 1 + x + 2)/((x + 2)(x − 1)) = (2x + 1)/((x + 2)(x − 1)).

A farmer's field has a total area of 6x² + 11x + 3 square yards. He wants to divide it into rectangular plots. If one dimension is (2x + 3), what is the other?

Factor 6x² + 11x + 3. Find factors of 18 (6 × 3) that add to 11: 9 and 2. Rewrite: 6x² + 9x + 2x + 3 = 3x(2x + 3) + 1(2x + 3) = (3x + 1)(2x + 3).
