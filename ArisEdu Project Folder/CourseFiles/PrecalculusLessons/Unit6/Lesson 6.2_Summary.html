Lesson 6.2: Arc Length & Sector Area - Summary

Key Concepts: Arc Length & Sector Area

Overview

This lesson covers the fundamental measures of center used to analyze and summarize data sets. You'll learn how to calculate and apply the mean, median, and mode, and understand when each is most appropriate.

Key Definitions

A circle has radius 8. What is the arc length subtended by a central angle of π/4?

s = rθ = 8 × π/4 = 2π.

A wheel of radius 3 ft rotates through an angle of 4 radians. What is the distance traveled by a point on the rim?

s = rθ = 3 × 4 = 12 ft.

If an arc length is 15 cm on a circle of radius 5 cm, what is the central angle in radians?

θ = s/r = 15/5 = 3 radians.

What is the angular speed (in rad/s) of a wheel that completes one full revolution in 4 seconds?

ω = θ/t = 2π/4 = π/2 rad/s.

A circle has circumference 20π. What is its radius?

C = 2πr, so 20π = 2πr, giving r = 10.

A wheel with radius 2 m has a linear speed of 6 m/s at the rim. What is the angular speed?

ω = v/r = 6/2 = 3 rad/s.

What is the formula for arc length when the central angle is given in radians?

Arc length s = rθ, where r is the radius and θ is the central angle in radians.

What is 'angular speed'?

Angular speed ω = θ/t measures how fast the angle changes, typically in radians per second.

What is the formula for the area of a sector when the angle is in radians?

The area of a sector is A = (1/2)r²θ, where θ must be in radians.

What is a 'central angle'?

A central angle has its vertex at the center of the circle and its sides are radii.

What is the relationship between linear speed v, radius r, and angular speed ω?

Linear speed equals the product of radius and angular speed: v = rω.

What is a 'segment' of a circle (as distinct from a sector)?

A segment is the region between a chord and the arc it cuts off, whereas a sector is bounded by radii.

A bicycle tire has a radius of 0.35 m. If the wheel rotates at 4 rad/s, what is the linear speed of a point on the rim?

v = rω = 0.35 × 4 = 1.4 m/s.

A pendulum swings through an arc of 18 cm on a string 45 cm long. What is the angle of the swing in radians?

θ = s/r = 18/45 = 0.4 radians.

A merry-go-round with radius 5 m makes one full revolution every 10 seconds. What is the linear speed of a child on the edge?

ω = 2π/10 = π/5 rad/s. v = rω = 5 × π/5 = π m/s.

A drone's camera covers a circular sector with radius 100 m and area 5000 m². What is the central angle of the sector in radians?

A = (1/2)r²θ → 5000 = (1/2)(10000)θ → θ = 1 radian.

A car tire with radius 0.3 m spins at 25 rev/s. What is the car's speed in m/s?

ω = 25 × 2π = 50π rad/s. v = rω = 0.3 × 50π = 15π m/s.

A record turntable spins at 33⅓ RPM. What is its angular speed in radians per second?

33⅓ RPM = 100/3 rev/min = (100/3)/60 rev/s = 5/9 rev/s. ω = (5/9)(2π) = 10π/9 rad/s.

Real-World Applications

Example: A windshield wiper blade is 20 inches long and sweeps through an angle of 2π/3 radians. What length of arc does the tip trace?

s = rθ = 20 × 2π/3 = 40π/3 inches.
