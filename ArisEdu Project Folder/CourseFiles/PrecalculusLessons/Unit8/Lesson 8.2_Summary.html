Lesson 8.2: Geometric Sequences (nth term, infinite series) - Summary

Key Concepts: Geometric Sequences (nth term, infinite series)

Overview

This lesson covers the fundamental measures of center used to analyze and summarize data sets. You'll learn how to calculate and apply the mean, median, and mode, and understand when each is most appropriate.

Key Definitions

What is the common ratio of the geometric sequence 3, 6, 12, 24, ...?

The common ratio r = 6/3 = 2. Each term is multiplied by 2.

What is the 8th term of the geometric sequence with a₁ = 5 and r = 2?

a₈ = 5 · 2⁷ = 5 · 128 = 640.

What is the formula for the nth term of a geometric sequence?

The nth term of a geometric sequence is aₙ = a₁ · rⁿ⁻¹, where a₁ is the first term and r is the common ratio.

What is the sum of the infinite geometric series 12 + 4 + 4/3 + 4/9 + ...?

a₁ = 12, r = 4/12 = 1/3. S = a₁/(1 − r) = 12/(1 − 1/3) = 12/(2/3) = 18.

What is the common ratio if a₃ = 36 and a₁ = 4?

a₃ = a₁ · r² → 36 = 4r² → r² = 9 → r = 3 (assuming positive ratio).

What is the 5th term of the geometric sequence 2, −6, 18, −54, ...?

r = −6/2 = −3. a₅ = 2(−3)⁴ = 2(81) = 162.

What is a 'geometric sequence'?

A geometric sequence has a constant ratio r between consecutive terms: aₙ₊₁ = aₙ · r.

What is a 'geometric series'?

A geometric series is the sum of the terms of a geometric sequence: S = a₁ + a₁r + a₁r² + ...

What is the 'geometric mean' of two positive numbers a and b?

The geometric mean of two positive numbers a and b is √(ab). In a geometric sequence, each interior term is the geometric mean of its neighbors.

What is 'exponential decay'?

Exponential decay occurs when a quantity is repeatedly multiplied by a factor r where 0 < r < 1, causing it to decrease toward zero.

What is an 'infinite geometric series'?

An infinite geometric series sums infinitely many terms: S = a₁ + a₁r + a₁r² + ... = a₁/(1−r) when |r| < 1.

A ball is dropped from a height of 10 meters and bounces back to 60% of its previous height each time. What is the total distance traveled before it comes to rest?

Total distance = 10 + 2(10)(0.6)/(1 − 0.6) = 10 + 2(10)(0.6)/0.4 = 10 + 30 = 40 meters (the initial drop plus the infinite series of bounces up and down).

A car depreciates at 15% per year. If the car is worth $30,000 new, what is it worth after 5 years?

Value = 30000(0.85)⁵ = 30000(0.4437) ≈ $13,311. Each year the car retains 85% of its value.

An investment of $5,000 earns 8% annual interest compounded annually. What is the value after 10 years?

A = 5000(1.08)¹⁰ = 5000(2.1589) ≈ $10,795.

A fractal pattern starts with a triangle of area 100 cm². At each step, the total new area added is 1/3 of the previous addition. What is the total area after infinitely many steps?

Total = 100 + 100(1/3) + 100(1/3)² + ... = 100/(1 − 1/3) = 100/(2/3) = 150 cm².

A company's revenue was $2 million in its first year and has grown by 20% each year. What is the total revenue over the first 5 years?

S₅ = 2(1.2⁵ − 1)/(1.2 − 1) = 2(2.48832 − 1)/0.2 = 2(1.48832)/0.2 = 2(7.4416) ≈ $14.88 million.

A pendulum swings through an arc of 80 cm on its first swing, with each subsequent swing being 90% as long. What is the total distance the pendulum travels before stopping?

S = 80/(1 − 0.9) = 80/0.1 = 800 cm.

A savings plan pays 5% interest compounded annually. If you deposit $1,000 at the start of each year for 20 years, what is the total value at the end of 20 years? (Use the annuity formula.)

Annuity due: FV = 1000 · [(1.05²⁰ − 1)/0.05] · 1.05 = 1000 · [33.066]/1 · 1.05 ≈ 1000 · 33.066 · 1.05 ≈ $34,719.
