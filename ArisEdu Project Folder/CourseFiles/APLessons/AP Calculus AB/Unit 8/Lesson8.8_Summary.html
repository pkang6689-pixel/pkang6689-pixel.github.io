Real-World Applications and Examples

This topic has significant real-world importance:

  • Quality control: using normal distribution integrals to determine the probability that a manufactured part falls within specifications (Six Sigma methodology)
  • Insurance: actuaries use probability integrals to calculate expected claim amounts and set premium prices
  • Weather forecasting: probability distributions of temperature, rainfall, and wind speed are computed via integration for probabilistic weather predictions
