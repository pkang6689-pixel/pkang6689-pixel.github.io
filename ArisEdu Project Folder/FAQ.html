Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ArisEdu.

General
What is ArisEdu? ▼

ArisEdu is an interactive educational platform designed to make learning engaging and accessible completely for free. We offer courses in Math, Science, and more, along with games and tools to support your studies.

Is ArisEdu free to use? ▼

Yes! ArisEdu is completely free for students and teachers. Parts of our mission is to ensure quality education is accessible to everyone.

Do I need an account? ▼

You can access most content without an account, but creating one allows you to track your progress, save preferences, earn achievements, and participate in the community forums.

Courses & Learning
What subjects are available? ▼

We currently offer comprehensive courses in Algebra 1, Algebra 2, Geometry, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. We are constantly expanding our curriculum.

How do I track my progress? ▼

Your progress is automatically saved when you are logged in. You can view your completion status, badges, and recent activity on the Dashboard.

Account & Settings
How do I change the language? ▼

Click on the Globe icon in the taskbar (top right) to switch between English, Spanish, Hindi, Chinese, and Traditional Chinese. The entire interface will update instantly.

Can I customize the theme? ▼

Yes! Go to Settings > Preferences to choose between different color themes like Sunset, Ocean, or Forest. You can also toggle Dark Mode from the settings dropdown.

Where can I find the Terms of Service? ▼

You can view our Terms of Service & Privacy Policy here.

Troubleshooting
The games aren't loading. What should I do? ▼

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or disabling ad blockers, as they sometimes interfere with game scripts.
