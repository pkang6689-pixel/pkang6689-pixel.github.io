Orbital Mechanics

Orbital velocity: v = √(GM/r). To go higher, burn prograde (forward). To go lower, burn retrograde. A Hohmann transfer uses two burns to move between circular orbits — it's the most fuel-efficient method. Escape velocity = √(2GM/r).
