Mineral: Quartz

Mineral Properties

Mohs Hardness (1-10): Scratch resistance (talc=1, diamond=10). Luster: How light reflects (metallic, vitreous, pearly, earthy). Streak: Color of powder on porcelain plate — often different from mineral color. Cleavage: How the mineral breaks along planes. These properties help geologists identify minerals in the field.
