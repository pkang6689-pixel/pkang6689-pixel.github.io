Collision Theory

For a reaction to occur, molecules must collide with sufficient energy (≥ activation energy Eₐ) and proper orientation. Higher temperature means faster molecules → more collisions above Eₐ. Higher concentration → more frequent collisions. A catalyst lowers Eₐ, allowing more collisions to succeed without being used up.
