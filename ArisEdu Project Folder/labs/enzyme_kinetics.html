Michaelis-Menten Equation: V₀ = Vmax·[S] / (Km + [S])

Vmax: Maximum reaction velocity when all enzyme active sites are saturated.
Km: Substrate concentration at half Vmax — a measure of enzyme affinity.
Competitive inhibitor: Increases apparent Km without changing Vmax.
Uncompetitive inhibitor: Decreases both apparent Vmax and Km.
