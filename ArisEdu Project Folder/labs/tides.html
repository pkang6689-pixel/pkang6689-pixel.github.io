Tidal Forces

Tides are caused by the gravitational pull of the Moon and Sun. Spring tides occur when Sun, Moon, and Earth align (new/full moon) — tidal range is maximum. Neap tides occur at quarter moons when forces partially cancel — minimum range. Earth experiences two high tides and two low tides per day due to the tidal bulge on both sides.
