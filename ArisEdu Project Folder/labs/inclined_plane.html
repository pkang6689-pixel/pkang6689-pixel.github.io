Inclined Plane Physics

On an incline, gravity splits into two components: F∥ = mg·sin(θ) pulls the block down the slope, F⊥ = mg·cos(θ) presses into the surface. Friction Ff = μ·FN opposes motion. The block accelerates if F∥ > Ff.
