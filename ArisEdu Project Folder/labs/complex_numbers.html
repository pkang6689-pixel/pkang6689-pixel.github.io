Complex Number Plane

Complex numbers z = a + bi are plotted on the Argand plane (real axis horizontal, imaginary vertical). Addition is vector addition. Multiplication multiplies magnitudes and adds angles: z₁z₂ = |z₁||z₂|∠(θ₁+θ₂). The conjugate z̄ = a − bi reflects across the real axis. Powers follow De Moivre: zⁿ = rⁿ(cos nθ + i sin nθ).
