Weather Fronts & Pressure Systems

Cold fronts (blue, triangles) bring rapid temperature drops and storms. Warm fronts (red, semicircles) bring steady rain then warming. Low-pressure systems have rising air → clouds and rain. High-pressure brings sinking air → clear skies. Hurricanes form over warm oceans (>26.5°C) with Coriolis-driven rotation.
