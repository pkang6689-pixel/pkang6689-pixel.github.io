Erosion Processes

River: Water carries sediment downstream, eroding V-shaped valleys and depositing deltas at the mouth. Wind: Abrasion carves desert features; sand accumulates as dunes. Glacial: Ice grinds U-shaped valleys, leaving moraines. All three transport and deposit material — erosion and deposition are linked.
