Mendelian Genetics

Each parent contributes one allele. Dominant alleles (uppercase) mask recessive (lowercase). A Punnett Square shows all possible offspring combinations. Heterozygous × Heterozygous → 1 AA : 2 Aa : 1 aa genotype ratio (3:1 phenotype ratio for complete dominance).
