Coulomb's Law & Electric Fields

Electric field E = kQ/r². Positive charges create outward field lines; negative charges pull field lines inward. The superposition principle: at any point, the net field is the vector sum of all individual fields. k ≈ 8.99 × 10⁹ N·m²/C².
